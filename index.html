Our Core Services

Co-Location

At our POP, we also lease space or even port(s) of equipment to customers for saving their O&M expenditure.

ICT

Provide network design, cabling, equipment procurement, installation, testing, acceptance, and handover to the customer as a turn-key mode.

Smart Hand

At our POP, the professional engineer can provide basic telecom services such as monitoring, cabling, testing, and more.

IPLC

IPLC provides flexibility and scalability to customers through customized dedicated point-to-point capacity solutions between two different locations internationally.

DPLC

With dedicated designed Domestic Private Leased Circuit, we can extend our service from our POP to the customer site.

Internet

Customize the internet content and provide it to partners or end-users at our POP and covered areas.

Our Network

WideAccel's network includes multiple submarine cables such as AAE-1, APG, and IA, providing extensive coverage from Cambodia to Hong Kong, Singapore, and beyond. Our international connectivity ensures seamless communication, backed by optical fiber routes, core routing, and switching to deliver high-speed and reliable services.
