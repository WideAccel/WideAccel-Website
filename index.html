Our Core Services

International Private Leased Circuit (IPLC)

Connecting international locations with dedicated point-to-point solutions, enabling seamless global communication with flexible bandwidth from 100M to 10G, including redundancy and protection options.

Domestic Private Leased Circuit (DPLC)

Reliable domestic connectivity between data centers and customer locations, ensuring stable backhaul connections across Hong Kong and Cambodia.

Virtual Private Network (VPN)

Ensuring secure remote access for businesses with a focus on data privacy and security.

Internet Access

Reliable and fast internet services that keep businesses connected to the world.

Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

End-to-end ICT solutions, including network design, cabling, equipment procurement, installation, testing, and maintenance, ensuring businesses have everything they need to succeed.

Capacity Wholesale

Wholesale bandwidth solutions across Hong Kong, Singapore, Cambodia, and other regions, offering up to 10G with redundancy for improved service reliability.
