Company Profile

Learn more about WideAccel and our journey.

Who We Are

WideAccel is an integrated telecommunications service provider licensed to act as a Telecom Wholesale Provider in Hong Kong. Our robust network and dedication enable us to meet the demands of local SMEs, foreign-invested enterprises, and VIP customers.

Global Connectivity

By partnering with tier-1 operators in Hong Kong, Singapore, Cambodia, Myanmar, and beyond, WideAccel has built an expansive telecommunications network that supports seamless connectivity across multiple countries.

Our Mission

Through efficient communication and professional service capabilities, we are committed to understanding and fulfilling the telecommunications requirements of our customers. Our mission is to drive growth, innovation, and connectivity worldwide.

Collaboration for a Shared Future

At WideAccel, we transcend the role of a mere service provider. As a strategic partner to telecom operators and enterprises, we collaborate to enhance communication, expand networks, and explore new business domains. Together, we aim to build a prosperous and innovative future.

About WideAccel

WideAccel is dedicated to providing innovative telecommunications services across Southeast Asia and beyond. Empowering businesses through reliable connectivity solutions.
