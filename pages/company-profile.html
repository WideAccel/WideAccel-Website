Company Overview

WideAccel is an integrated telecommunications service provider licensed to act as a Telecom Wholesale Provider in Hong Kong. To cooperate with tier-1 operators in Hong Kong, Singapore, Cambodia, Myanmar, and across the world, WideAccel has built a telecommunications network covering multiple countries.

Our network primarily serves local SMEs, foreign-invested enterprises with internet as their main demand, and VIP customers in need of Premium Telecom Services.

Through efficient communication and professional service capabilities, we quickly understand and fulfill customer needs for any telecommunications requirements.
